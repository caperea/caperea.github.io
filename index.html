    Privacy Policy

    This Privacy Policy aims to transparently explain how Caperea ("we", "our") collects, uses, and protects the information of users ("you", "your") of Calculator HD ("the app").

    1. Data Collection and Use

    We are committed to protecting your privacy and data. We do not collect any personal information directly from the app. All data collection relies on the anonymous statistical data collection mechanism provided by the iOS operating system. This may include anonymized information such as app usage frequency, features used, and app crash reports, aimed at helping us improve app performance and user experience.

    2. Data Sharing

    We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to external parties. The anonymous statistical data collection we rely on strictly adheres to Apple's privacy policy and guidelines.

    3. Data Protection

    We take appropriate security measures to protect the data stored and processed, to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

    4. Third-Party Links

    Our app may contain links to third-party websites or services, which have privacy practices independent from us. Therefore, we bear no responsibility for the content or privacy policies of these links. We encourage you to read the privacy policy of every website you visit.

    5. Changes to Our Privacy Policy

    We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify of any changes through the app or our website. Your continued use of the app will be deemed as your acceptance of those changes.

    6. Contact Us

    If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at badtoyx@gmail.com.
